Welcome to ELIG!

We are people who like words! You heard that right! Words!

We have 4 divisions

Elysium: Members focus on appreciating literature by writing and discussing.
They organise activities such as the Creative Writing Circle and Poetry recital.

Lexicon: Lexicon memeber run Semantics Smackdown, linguistics puzzles, and NUSH's
very own linguistics league. The members play scrabble and explore linguistics based games.

Insight: Insight members gain deeper appreciation of current affairs.
They run a termly edition of Insightful Insights to the school and hold insight buzz sessions!

Genesis is the division for communication. We host public speaking workshops and hold rap battles

We believe that knowledge is power. But language empowers.

We believe that language is a keystone in the field of STEM!
